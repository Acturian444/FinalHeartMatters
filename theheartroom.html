The Heart Room is a live confessional talk show where strangers say what they've never said out loud. Broadcast on TikTok, it's where people walk through a virtual red door and finally speak the truths they've kept hidden — stories of shame, heartbreak, grief, guilt, and raw emotion.

No scripts. No filters. Just truth.

This is where silence ends, and healing begins.
